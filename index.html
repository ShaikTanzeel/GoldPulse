⚠️ Risk Rules for $50 Account

  • 🎯
    Max 1-2% risk per trade

    That's $0.50-$1.00 per trade. Discipline is survival.

  • 📏
    Micro lots only (0.01)

    On XM Ultra Low, 0.01 lot on XAUUSD ≈ $0.01/pip. Perfect for small accounts.

  • ⚖️
    Minimum 1:2 Risk:Reward

    Never take a trade where potential reward is less than 2x your risk.

  • 🚫
    Max 1 trade at a time

    With $50, one open position is enough. No overexposure.

  • 📅
    Avoid news events

    NFP, FOMC, CPI — close or don't open positions before high-impact events.
